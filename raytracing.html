Powers realistic Rendering

Raytracing provides realistic images by simulating a light ray's path. This creates more realistic images than the more common for of rendering, Rasterization.

Simulates Shutter Effect

By simulating these light rays, raytracing provides an easy way to simulate camera or eye exposure. This makes raytraced images even more realistic.

All this realism comes at a cost: Computer performance. Raytracing is one of the most calculation intensive forms of rendering there is.
