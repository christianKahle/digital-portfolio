Field of Vision

Image courtesy of Unity Answers.
The field of vision (FOV), also known the view frustum is the area that gets projected onto the screen during rendering.

The view view frustum is essentially a pyramid made of five planes: left, right, top, bottom, and back.

Most programs treat the FOV as a number that represents the angle between the left and right planes.
